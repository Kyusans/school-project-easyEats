Peanut Butter Banana Toast

Ingredients Amount
Bread 1 Slice
Peanut butter 1 tablespoon
Banana 1/2, sliced
Honey 1 teaspoon
Directions:
  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on the toast.
  3. Cut the banana into thin slices and arrange them on top of the peanut butter.
  4. Drizzle with honey, if desired.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
